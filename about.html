About Spokane Balloon Dude

Hi there! I'm Eric Polley, the Spokane Balloon Dude. I specialize in creating fun and exciting balloon sculptures for all kinds of events. Whether you're hosting a birthday party, a corporate event, or a community gathering, I bring the fun with custom-made balloon animals and creations that will bring joy to everyone.

I strive to provide unique, high-quality balloon art that adds a special touch to any occasion. From simple balloon animals to intricate designs, I take pride in my work and love making people smile.

When I'm not twisting balloons, I'm always looking for new ways to improve my craft and provide the best service to my clients. I believe in offering a fun, friendly, and professional experience for every event, ensuring that your event is memorable for all the right reasons.

If you're looking for an entertainer who can make your event truly unforgettable, look no further! Let me bring some balloon magic to your special occasion.
