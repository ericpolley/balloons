Balloon Twisting Pricing

We offer a fun, interactive, and colorful balloon twisting experience for your events. Here's a breakdown of our pricing:

Standard Pricing

$50 for 20 Balloon Drop-off

$100 Per Hour for In-Person Balloon Twisting for Events!

Please note: A minimum of 1 hour is required for all IN PERSON events.

Conditions That May Impact Pricing or Balloon Art Quality:

  • Weather conditions, especially extreme heat, extreme cold, rain, snow, or wind, may affect the quality and durability of balloon art.
  • High temperatures can cause balloons to deflate or pop quicker, potentially requiring additional supplies or time.
  • Travel fees may apply depending on the event's location outside of Spokane.
  • Additional costs may arise for very large events or additional customization of balloon art.

If you're ready to book or need more details, feel free to contact us!
